# SnapBlocks: Marketing & Brand Specification

## 📢 Brand Voice
*   **Playful:** It’s a game first, learning second.
*   **Tactile:** We emphasize the "Snap" and the "Feel" of the blocks.
*   **Encouraging:** Every success is a celebration.
*   **Modern:** Using the latest web tech for a premium mobile experience.

---

## 🖼 Poster & Ad Prompts

### Concept A: "The Tactile Toy" (Product Focus)
**Visual:** 3D close-up of a hand 'snapping' a glowing blue 'B' block into a frosted glass slot.
**Copy:** "Learning you can feel. Snap. Build. Bloom."
**AI Prompt:** `Close-up macro photography, 3D render, a child's finger touching a glowing translucent toy block with the letter 'B', frosted glass texture, soft bokeh background, 8k resolution, Unreal Engine 5 style.`

### Concept B: "The Learning Universe" (Atmospheric)
**Visual:** A galaxy where planets are shaped like blocks and equations float like stars.
**Copy:** "A Universe of Words and Numbers. Explore SnapBlocks."
**AI Prompt:** `Whimsical space galaxy, planets made of colorful building blocks, floating mathematical symbols and letters as constellations, vibrant purple and blue nebula, paper-cut art style mixed with 3D glassmorphism, high detail.`
